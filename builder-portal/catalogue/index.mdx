## Why use the catalogue?

Building a comprehensive catalogue helps you:

- **Save time** — Reuse content instead of recreating it
- **Ensure consistency** — Standard descriptions and pricing
- **Reduce errors** — Pre-vetted content with correct information
- **Scale efficiently** — New team members can use proven content

## Getting started

### Build your item library

Start by adding your most commonly used:

- Products with standard pricing
- Service offerings
- Materials and finishes
- Fixtures and appliances

### Add images

Upload photos and product images:

- Product photos
- Inspiration images
- Detail shots
- Branded graphics

### Create text blocks

Write reusable content for:

- Scope descriptions
- Standard notes
- Disclaimers
- Process explanations

### Define terms

Set up your contract language:

- Payment terms
- Warranty terms
- General conditions
- Specific provisions

<Tip>
Invest time building your catalogue early. The time saved on future projects will be substantial.
</Tip>

## Using catalogue content

Catalogue items can be inserted into:

- Proposals and estimates
- Selections and allowances
- Change orders
- Project documentation

<Note>
Catalogue content belongs to your organization and is available across all projects.
</Note>
