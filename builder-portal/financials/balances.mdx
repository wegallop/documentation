The Balances section shows outstanding amounts owed across all your projects. Quickly identify who owes what and prioritize collection efforts.

## Balance overview

View all project balances:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Project** | Project name |
| **Client** | Client name |
| **Invoiced** | Total amount invoiced |
| **Paid** | Total payments received |
| **Balance** | Outstanding amount |
| **Aging** | How long outstanding |

## Balance status

Balances are categorized by age:

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Current** | Not yet due |
| **1-30 days** | Recently past due |
| **31-60 days** | Moderately overdue |
| **60-90 days** | Significantly overdue |
| **90+ days** | Seriously overdue |

## Viewing balance details

Click on any balance to see:

- All invoices for the project
- Payment history
- Invoice status breakdown
- Contact information
- Communication history

## Filtering balances

Filter by:

- **Client** — Specific client
- **Aging** — By age category
- **Amount** — By balance range
- **Project status** — Active or complete

## Balance metrics

Track across your organization:

- Total outstanding
- Current vs. overdue
- Aging breakdown
- Collection rate

## Taking action

From the balance view:

- Send payment reminders
- View invoice details
- Record received payments
- Add notes

<Tip>
Focus collection efforts on the oldest balances first. Regular follow-up improves cash flow significantly.
</Tip>

## Collection workflow

Suggested process for overdue balances:

1. **30 days** — Friendly payment reminder
2. **45 days** — Phone call follow-up
3. **60 days** — Formal collection notice
4. **90+ days** — Escalate to management

<Note>
Maintain professional communication with clients about overdue balances. Understanding their situation often leads to successful resolution.
</Note>
