## Financial oversight

The Financials section helps you:

- Monitor cash flow across all projects
- Track outstanding receivables
- Review payment schedules and upcoming invoices
- Analyze project profitability using budgets, forecasts, and actuals
- Generate internal and client‑facing financial reports

## Key metrics

At a glance, view:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Total invoiced** | Sum of all invoices across projects |
| **Total received** | Payments received to date |
| **Outstanding** | Total amount owed |
| **Scheduled** | Upcoming scheduled invoices and credit memos |

## Navigation

Use the sidebar to access different financial views:

- Dashboard for overview
- Individual sections for detailed data
- Filters to narrow by project, date, or status

<Note>
Access to financial sections may be restricted based on your role. Administrators have full access to all financial data.
</Note>
