The Payment Schedules section shows all payment schedules from across your projects. Monitor upcoming payments and track schedule adherence.

## Schedule overview

View all payment schedules with:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Project** | Associated project |
| **Client** | Client name |
| **Schedule type** | Fixed date, milestone-based |
| **Total value** | Total scheduled amount |
| **Paid** | Amount paid to date |
| **Remaining** | Amount still outstanding |

## Upcoming payments

See payments due soon across all projects:

- Today's payments
- This week's payments
- This month's payments

## Schedule status

Payment schedules show their status:

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **On track** | Payments being made as scheduled |
| **Behind** | One or more payments are late |
| **Complete** | All payments received |

## Viewing schedule details

Click on any schedule to see:

- All scheduled payments
- Payment dates
- Amounts due
- Linked milestones (if applicable)
- Payment status

## Filtering schedules

Filter by:

- **Project** — Specific project
- **Client** — Specific client
- **Status** — On track, behind, complete
- **Due date** — Upcoming time period

## Schedule metrics

Track across your organization:

- Total scheduled value
- Total collected
- Upcoming 30-day forecast
- Overdue amounts

<Tip>
Review upcoming payments weekly and proactively reach out to clients about payments due. This improves cash flow and client relationships.
</Tip>

## Taking action

From this view, you can:

- View full schedule details
- See payment history
- Access related invoices
- Send payment reminders

<Note>
Payment schedules are created and managed within individual projects. This section provides the organization-wide view.
</Note>
