The Inbox consolidates communications and tasks from across your organization. Instead of checking each project individually, view everything that needs your attention in one place.

## Messages overview

The Messages section shows conversations from all your projects:

- Project chats
- Direct messages
- Decision threads
- Client communications

### Filtering messages

Filter conversations by:

- **Project** — Show messages from specific projects
- **Unread** — Only show unread conversations
- **Type** — Filter by conversation type

### Message indicators

Visual indicators help identify message status:

- **Unread badge** — New messages waiting
- **Project label** — Which project the conversation belongs to
- **Participant avatars** — Who's in the conversation

## Tasks overview

The Tasks section aggregates tasks requiring attention:

- Tasks assigned to you
- Tasks you've assigned to others
- Overdue tasks
- Upcoming deadlines

### Task views

- **My Tasks** — Tasks assigned to you
- **Assigned by Me** — Tasks you've delegated
- **Team Tasks** — All team tasks

<Tip>
Check your Inbox at the start of each day to stay on top of communications and tasks across all your projects.
</Tip>

## Notifications

The Inbox shows notification counts:

- Unread message count on the Messages tab
- Pending task count on the Tasks tab
- Total notifications on the Inbox menu item

<Note>
Configure your notification preferences in Settings to control how you're alerted to new messages and tasks.
</Note>
