## Navigation

The sidebar provides quick access to all sections of the Builder Portal. The main navigation includes:

- **Projects** — Active and archived projects
- **Inbox** — Messages and tasks
- **Clients** — Client management
- **Contracting** — Proposals and change orders
- **Financials** — Financial dashboard and reports
- **Requests** — Change and warranty requests
- **Catalogue** — Reusable items and content
- **Templates** — Document templates
- **Users** — Team management (admin only)
- **Settings** — Configuration options

## Getting started

If you're new to the platform, start with these essential tasks:

1. **Configure your organization** — Set up your branding and business details in Settings
2. **Add team members** — Invite your team and assign roles
3. **Build your catalogue** — Add items, images, and text you'll reuse
4. **Create templates** — Set up templates for proposals and selections
5. **Add clients** — Build your client database
6. **Create your first project** — Start managing work

<Note>
Some sections are only available to administrators. Team members will see sections based on their assigned permissions.
</Note>
