Account settings control your personal profile, security, and notification preferences. These settings apply only to your account and influence how Gallop communicates with you across projects and devices.

## Profile settings

### Personal information

Update your profile details:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Profile photo
- Job title

### Display preferences

Customize your experience:

- Time zone
- Date format
- Language (if available)

## Security settings

### Password

Manage your password:

- Change password
- Password requirements
- Last changed date

### Two-factor authentication

Add an extra layer of security:

1. Enable 2FA
2. Scan QR code with authenticator app
3. Enter verification code
4. Save backup codes

<Tip>
Enable two-factor authentication for better account security. Store backup codes safely in case you lose access to your authenticator.
</Tip>

### Sessions

View and manage active sessions:

- Current session
- Other devices
- Log out of all sessions

## Notification preferences

Control how and when you receive notifications:

### Email notifications

- New messages
- Task assignments
- Decision requests
- Financial updates
- System notifications

### In-app notifications

- Real-time alerts
- Badge counts
- Sound preferences

### Notification frequency

Choose how often to receive digest emails:

- Immediately
- Hourly digest
- Daily digest
- Never

## Setting up notifications

For each notification type, choose:

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Email** | Send to your email |
| **In-app** | Show in application |
| **Both** | Email and in-app |
| **None** | Disable notification |

<Note>
Some critical notifications cannot be disabled to ensure you receive important information.
</Note>

## Saving changes

Changes to account settings save automatically or when you click Save, depending on the setting.

## Account support

If you have issues with your account:

- Password reset via email
- Contact your organization admin
- Check help documentation
