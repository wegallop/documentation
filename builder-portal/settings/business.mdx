Business settings define your organization's identity and appearance across the platform. These settings affect all users and how clients see your company in proposals, change orders, invoices, the client portal, and emails.

<Note>
Business settings are only accessible to administrators.
</Note>

## Company information

### Basic details

- **Legal name** — Registered legal business name.
- **Operating name** — Name shown to clients (if different).
- **Addresses** — Legal, mailing, and remittance addresses.
- **Phone** — Main contact number.
- **Email** — Primary email address for client communication.
- **Website** — Company website.

### Business details

- **Business type** — Entity type.
- **Registration numbers** — License and registration details.
- **Insurance** — Insurance information.
- **Tax / business numbers** — For financial documents and invoicing.

## Branding

### Logo and imagery

Upload your company logos and images:

- **Primary logo** — Main logo for light backgrounds.
- **Alternate logo** — Optional logo for dark backgrounds.
- **Icon** — Square icon or app icon.
- **Login banner** — Optional image for your branded client login experience.

Use high‑quality SVG or PNG files with transparent backgrounds where possible.

### Colors

Set your brand colors:

- **Primary color** — Main brand color.
- **Accent color** — Secondary color.

These colors appear throughout the platform and in client‑facing documents (proposals, change orders, invoices, and the client portal).

<Tip>
Use your official brand colors for consistency with your other marketing materials.
</Tip>

## Document branding

Customize how your documents appear:

### Proposals

- Header/footer content
- Logo placement
- Color scheme

### Invoices

- Company details
- Payment information
- Terms and notes

### Contracts

- Standard header
- Signature block
- Legal footer

## Email branding

Customize outgoing emails:

- From name
- Reply-to address
- Email signature
- Logo in emails

## Saving changes

After making changes:

1. Review all updates
2. Click **Save**
3. Changes apply immediately

<Note>
Branding changes affect all new documents. Existing documents retain their original branding.
</Note>
