## Account settings

Personal settings that apply to your account:

- Profile information
- Password and security
- Notification preferences

## Business settings

Organization-wide settings (admin only):

- Company information and legal details.
- Branding, logos, and login banner.
- Business details, tax numbers, and remittance information.

## Configuration

System settings that control how the platform works (admin only):

- Job titles and departments
- Categories and groups
- Project attributes
- Cost codes
- Payment methods
- Tags

## Integrations

Connect with external systems (admin only):

- Accounting software (for example, QuickBooks Online).
- Payment processors.
- Other business tools.

## Access levels

Setting access depends on your role:

| Section | Team Member | Manager | Admin |
|---------|-------------|---------|-------|
| Account | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Business | — | — | ✓ |
| Configuration | — | — | ✓ |
| Integrations | — | — | ✓ |

<Tip>
Take time to configure settings properly when you first set up your organization. Good configuration makes daily work much smoother.
</Tip>

## Making changes

When modifying settings:

1. Navigate to the relevant section
2. Make your changes
3. Save (changes apply immediately)

<Note>
Some settings changes affect all users in your organization. Review changes carefully before saving.
</Note>
