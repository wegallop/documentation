## Financial overview

From the Financials section, you can:

- View your current balance
- Review past invoices and payments
- See upcoming scheduled payments
- Track allowance spending against budgets
- Compare estimates with actual costs where available

## Understanding your balance

Your project balance reflects:

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Contract amount** | Original contract value |
| **Change orders** | Approved changes and additions |
| **Invoiced** | Total amount billed to date |
| **Paid** | Payments received |
| **Balance due** | Outstanding amount |

<Note>
Access to financial information depends on your project permissions. Contact your team if you need access to specific financial details.
</Note>

## Making payments

Payment options vary by project. Your team will provide instructions for:

- Online payments
- Bank transfers
- Check payments
- Payment schedules

<Tip>
Keep track of payment due dates using the Payment Schedule section to avoid late fees or project delays.
</Tip>
