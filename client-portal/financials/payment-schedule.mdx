The Payment Schedule shows your agreed‑upon payment plan, helping you prepare for upcoming payments throughout your project. It brings together scheduled invoices and credit memos so you can see what is coming up at a glance.

## Understanding payment schedules

Your payment schedule outlines when payments are due. Schedules can be:

- **Milestone‑based** — Payments due when specific work is completed.
- **Fixed date** — Payments due on specific calendar dates.
- **Progress‑based** — Regular payments based on work completed.

## Schedule view

The payment schedule displays:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Payment** | Description or name of the payment |
| **Amount** | Payment amount due |
| **Due date** | When payment is required |
| **Status** | Current state (upcoming, due, paid) |
| **Milestone** | Associated project milestone (if applicable) |

## Payment status

Each scheduled payment shows its current status:

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Upcoming** | Future payment not yet due |
| **Due** | Payment is currently due |
| **Overdue** | Payment is past due |
| **Paid** | Payment has been received |

## Multiple schedules

Some projects may have multiple payment schedules for different portions of work:

- Main contract payments
- Change order payments
- Allowance adjustments

You can view each schedule separately or see a combined view.

## Preparing for payments

<Tip>
Review your payment schedule at the start of each month to plan for upcoming payments. This helps avoid surprises and ensures your project stays on track.
</Tip>

## Schedule adjustments

Payment schedules may be adjusted when:

- Project milestones shift
- Change orders are approved
- Contract terms are modified

Your team will communicate any changes to your payment schedule.

<Note>
If you need to discuss payment terms or request adjustments to your schedule, contact your project manager through the Messages section.
</Note>

## Making payments

When a payment is due:

1. Review the amount and what it covers
2. Follow your team's payment instructions
3. Keep confirmation for your records
4. Payment will be reflected in your Statement
