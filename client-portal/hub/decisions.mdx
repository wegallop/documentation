The Decisions section is where you'll find all items requiring your input or approval. This includes selections (choosing finishes, fixtures, etc.), client tasks, and proposals.

## Decision types

### Selections

Selections are curated choices you need to make for your project, such as:

- Finishes (paint colors, flooring, tiles).
- Fixtures (faucets, lighting, hardware).
- Appliances.
- Materials and fittings.

Each selection presents one or more options with images, descriptions, and (where applicable) pricing. Some selections are linked to allowances so you can see how your choices affect your allowance budgets as you decide.

### Tasks

Tasks are action items assigned to you. These might include:

- Providing information.
- Reviewing documents.
- Completing forms or checklists.
- Making approvals or confirmations.

### Proposals

Proposals present estimates or quotes for additional work or changes to your project. You can review the details, ask questions, and approve or decline proposals directly from this section.

## Viewing decisions

Decisions are organized into tabs:

| Tab | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| **Open** | Active decisions awaiting your input |
| **Closed** | Completed decisions |
| **All** | View all decisions regardless of status |
| **Archived** | Archived decisions (builder access only) |

## Filtering and organizing

Use the filters to find specific decisions:

- **Type** — Filter by Selections, Tasks, or Proposals
- **Status** — Filter by decision status
- **Group by** — Organize by Category, Room, or None

## Making a selection

1. Click on a selection to open the detail view
2. Review the available options, including images and descriptions
3. Click on your preferred option to select it
4. Confirm your selection

<Tip>
You can leave comments or ask questions about any decision before making your choice. Use the chat feature to communicate with your project team.
</Tip>

## Responding to tasks

1. Click on the task to view details
2. Review any instructions or requirements
3. Complete the requested action
4. Submit your response

## Reviewing proposals

1. Click on the proposal to view the full details
2. Review the scope, pricing, and terms
3. Ask questions if needed using the chat feature
4. Approve or decline the proposal
