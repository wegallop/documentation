The Hub is your command center for managing all aspects of your project. It brings together decisions, requests, activity logs, and team information in one organized space.

## Sections

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Decisions" icon="clipboard-check" href="/client-portal/hub/decisions">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Requests" icon="file-lines" href="/client-portal/hub/requests">
    Change requests for your project
  </Card>
  <Card title="Warranty Requests" icon="shield-check" href="/client-portal/hub/warranty-requests">
    Track warranty claims and repairs
  </Card>
  <Card title="Logs" icon="list" href="/client-portal/hub/logs">
    Project activity and notes
  </Card>
  <Card title="Team" icon="users" href="/client-portal/hub/team">
    View your project team members
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Navigation

The Hub sidebar provides quick access to all sections. A badge on the Hub tab shows the number of open decisions requiring your attention.

<Note>
Some sections like Requests, Warranty Requests, and Logs may be hidden based on your project configuration and permissions.
</Note>
