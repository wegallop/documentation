The Team section displays everyone involved in your project. This gives you a clear understanding of who's working on your project and their roles.

## Team member information

For each team member, you can view:

- **Name** — Team member's name
- **Role** — Their position or job title
- **Department** — The department they belong to
- **Contact** — How to reach them (if enabled)

## Team structure

Your project team may include:

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Project Manager** | Main point of contact for your project |
| **Designer** | Handles design decisions and selections |
| **Site Supervisor** | Oversees on-site construction |
| **Estimator** | Handles pricing and proposals |
| **Administrator** | Manages scheduling and coordination |

<Note>
Team structure varies by company. Your specific team may have different roles and titles.
</Note>

## Contacting your team

From the Team section, you can:

- View team member profiles
- See their role and responsibilities
- Access contact information (if provided)
- Send direct messages through the Messages feature

<Tip>
For project questions, it's often best to use the project chat in the Messages section. This keeps all communication in one place and visible to the relevant team members.
</Tip>

## Key contacts

Your project typically has designated contacts for different types of questions:

- **General questions** — Project Manager
- **Design decisions** — Designer
- **Site concerns** — Site Supervisor
- **Financial questions** — Administrator or Project Manager
