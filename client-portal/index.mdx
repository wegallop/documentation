## Project dashboard

When you first enter a project, you'll see your project dashboard which provides a quick overview of:

- **Project details** — Project name, image, and team members
- **Up next** — Quick links to open decisions and unread messages
- **Balance** — Your current financial standing (if enabled)
- **Schedule snapshot** — A visual timeline of upcoming phases and milestones
- **Recent uploads** — The latest files added to your project

## Navigation

The main navigation appears at the top of the screen with tabs for each section:

- **Home** — Project dashboard and overview
- **Hub** — Decisions, requests, logs, and team
- **Financials** — Statements, payments, and allowances
- **Schedule** — Timeline, critical path, and briefings
- **Files** — All project files and documents
- **Messages** — Project chat and direct messages

<Note>
Some sections may be hidden based on your permissions. Contact your project team if you need access to additional features.
</Note>
