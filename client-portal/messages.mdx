The Messages section is your communication hub for the project. Keep all project conversations in one place, making it easy to reference past discussions and stay connected with your team without relying on separate email or text threads.

## Chat types

### Project chat

The main project chat is visible to all participants with access to your project. Use this for:

- General questions.
- Updates and announcements.
- Non‑urgent communication that others may find helpful.

### Direct messages

One‑on‑one conversations with specific team members. Use for:

- Private questions.
- Personal matters.
- Specific follow‑ups.

### Decision threads

Conversations linked to specific decisions (selections, tasks, proposals, or requests). These keep discussions organized and connected to the relevant item so you can see context and conversation together.

## Using messages

### Sending a message

1. Open the Messages section
2. Select the conversation or start a new one
3. Type your message in the text box
4. Click **Send** or press Enter

### Attaching files

You can share files directly in messages:

1. Click the attachment icon
2. Select files from your device
3. Add your message
4. Send

### Unread indicators

A notification badge shows when you have unread messages. The badge appears on:

- The Messages tab
- Individual conversations with new messages

## Best practices

<Tip>
Use the project chat for questions that might benefit others on the team. This keeps everyone informed and reduces duplicate questions.
</Tip>

### Keep context in threads

When discussing a specific decision or selection, use the thread attached to that item. This keeps the conversation connected to the relevant context.

### Be specific

Include relevant details in your messages:

- Reference the specific item or area
- Attach photos if helpful
- Mention the relevant team member

### Response times

Your team will respond as quickly as possible. For urgent matters, follow up with a phone call using the contact information provided.

## Notifications

You'll receive notifications for new messages through:

- In-app notification badges
- Email notifications (if enabled)
- Mobile push notifications (if using the app)

<Note>
Notification preferences can be adjusted in your account settings.
</Note>

## Viewing linked items

When a conversation is linked to a decision, selection, or other item:

- A link appears at the top of the chat
- Click the link to view the related item
- Return to the chat using the back button

This makes it easy to reference what you're discussing while keeping the conversation organized.
