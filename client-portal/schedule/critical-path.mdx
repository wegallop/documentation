The Critical Path provides a comprehensive visual timeline of your entire project. This Gantt‑style chart shows all phases, milestones, permits, and inspections in relation to each other so you can see how work connects over time.

## Understanding the Gantt chart

The chart displays your project timeline horizontally, with:

- **Time axis** — Dates shown across the top
- **Vertical bars** — Each item spans its duration
- **Today marker** — A line showing the current date

## Schedule items

### Phases

Phases appear as horizontal bars spanning their duration. Each phase represents a major stage of work:

- Foundation
- Framing
- Rough-ins (electrical, plumbing, HVAC)
- Insulation and drywall
- Finishing
- Final walkthrough

### Milestones

Milestones appear as markers on specific dates, representing key achievements:

- Design approval
- Permit approval
- Substantial completion
- Final inspection
- Project handover

### Permits

Permits are tracked from application through approval:

- Application date
- Review period
- Approval date

### Inspections

Required inspections are shown at their scheduled dates:

- Foundation inspection
- Framing inspection
- Electrical inspection
- Plumbing inspection
- Final inspection

## View options

You can adjust how you view the timeline:

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Zoom** | Adjust the time scale (days, weeks, months) |
| **List view** | Switch to a list format |
| **Filter** | Show/hide specific item types |

## Reading the chart

<Tip>
Hover over any item to see its full details, including dates, status, and description.
</Tip>

### Progress indicators

- **Filled bars** — Work completed
- **Empty bars** — Work remaining
- **Partial fill** — Work in progress

### Dependencies

Some items depend on others completing first. The chart may show these relationships with connecting lines, helping you understand the sequence of work.

## Tracking changes

If your project schedule changes, you'll see:

- Updated dates reflected in the chart
- Shift in dependent items
- Notes explaining the change (if provided)

<Note>
Schedule changes can occur due to weather, material availability, inspections, or change requests. Your team will communicate significant changes to you.
</Note>
