## Schedule components

Your project schedule includes several types of items:

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Phases** | Major stages of your project (e.g., Foundation, Framing, Finishing) |
| **Milestones** | Key dates and deliverables |
| **Inspections** | Required inspections throughout construction |
| **Permits** | Permit applications and approvals |

## Staying informed

The Schedule section helps you:

- Understand what's happening this week and month
- Plan around key milestones and inspections
- Track progress against the original timeline
- Prepare for upcoming phases of work

<Note>
Access to the Schedule section depends on your project permissions. Contact your team if you need access.
