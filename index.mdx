## What is Gallop?

Gallop is a client‑first construction project platform built for design‑build firms and developers. It brings proposals, selections, financials, scheduling, and communication into one experience that keeps your internal team and your clients aligned from first proposal through warranty.

Gallop is designed for organizations that own the client relationship end‑to‑end. You use it to estimate and sell work, manage day‑to‑day decisions, track project performance, and give clients a clear, calm view of what is happening on their project.

## Core principles

- **Client‑first philosophy**: You design the internal workflows you need while giving your clients a transparent, friendly portal for decisions, money, and communication.
- **Purpose‑built for design‑build**: Projects, financials, and decisions reflect how design‑build firms actually run jobs – from early concept through construction, handover, and warranty.
- **Unified workspace**: Your team, your clients, and your data live in one place instead of being fragmented across email threads, spreadsheets, and disconnected tools.

## What Gallop covers

Gallop is organized around a few major areas of your business:

- **Core framework**: Projects, clients, builder users, and a flexible permission system that matches your org structure.
- **Accounting & financials**: Deep project accounting, client‑facing financial transparency, and cross‑project financial views.
- **Decision engine**: Proposals and change orders, selections, client tasks, and formal change/warranty requests.
- **Collaboration**: Schedule, briefings, logs, files, and chat that keep everyone aligned without noisy email threads.
- **Platform**: Org‑level settings, catalogue, integrations, notifications, and the native mobile app.

Use the navigation to explore each area in more detail, or start with the **Quickstart** if you are setting up Gallop for the first time.
